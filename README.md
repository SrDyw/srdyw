## 🤖 AI-Powered Development — Done Right

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://readme-typing-svg.herokuapp.com?font=Fira+Code&color=A855F7&size=16&center=true&vCenter=true&width=700&height=50&lines=AI+is+a+tool.+Critical+thinking+is+the+skill." alt="AI quote">
</p>

I leverage AI tools like **ChatGPT**, **Claude**, and **OpenCode** to deliver results at a completely different level — but the thinking is always mine.

Here's how I actually use AI:

- 🧠 **I lead, AI assists** — I define the architecture, the logic, the decisions. AI accelerates execution, not replaces judgment.
- 🔍 **Critical review always** — I never blindly paste AI output. I read it, understand it, challenge it, and adapt it to the real context of the project.
- ⚡ **200% productivity** — combining my technical knowledge with AI tools lets me ship faster, debug smarter, and explore more solutions in less time.
- 🧪 **Learning amplifier** — I use AI to go deeper into topics, not to skip understanding them.

> *"A hammer doesn't build a house — the person holding it does. AI is my hammer."*
